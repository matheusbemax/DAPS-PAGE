Become a GEM Member!

Dallas Area Parkinsonism Society (DAPS) GEMs are donors who Give Every Month. Our GEMs are very valuable! DAPS provides free programs and services to all people affected by Parkinson’s disorders, such as Parkinson's disease (PD), PSP, CBD, MSA, and DLB. Monthly donations are vital for funding these services.

Diamond $100+: Your gift of $100 or more per month will pay for two speech classes per month.

Ruby: Your gift of $75 per month will pay for one care partner support group each month, led by trained professionals.

Emerald: Your gift of $50 per month will pay for one exercise group per month – enhancing strength, balance, and community for people with PD.

Amethyst: Your gift of $10 per month will provide funds for exercise equipment for people with PD.

Amethyst $5-$24: Your gift of $5 to $24 per month will provide funds for equipment for our 10+ weekly exercise groups specifically for people with Parkinson’s.
