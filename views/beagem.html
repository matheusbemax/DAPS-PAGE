DAPS GEM

Dallas Area Parkinsonism Society (DAPS) GEMs are donors who Give Every Month. Our GEMs are very valuable! DAPS provides free programs and services to all people affected by Parkinson’s disorders, such as Parkinson's disease (PD), PSP, CBD, MSA, and DLB. Monthly donations are vital for funding these services.

Diamond $100+: Your gift of $100 or more per month will pay for a full year’s worth of fees for the space where we provide our monthly educational/inspirational meetings.

Ruby $75-$99: Your gift of $74 to $99 per month will pay for one care partner support group each month - a lifeline for caregivers.

Emerald $50-$74: Your gift of $50 to $74 per month will pay for one exercise group each month - enhancing strength and balance for people with PD.

Sapphire $25-$49: Your gift of $25 to $49 per month will provide for one Dance for Movement Disorders class each month - a musical alternative for regular exercise.

Amethyst $5-$24: Your gift of $5 to $24 per month will provide funds for equipment for our 10+ weekly exercise groups specifically for people with Parkinson’s.
