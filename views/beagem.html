Become a GEM Member!

GEM (donors who ive very onth) and annual membership dues provide critical funding which allows DAPS to provide FREE services throughout the Dallas metroplex:

  • Exercise/speech classes
  • Support groups led by professionals
  • Educational series
  • Dance for PD classes
  • Monthly newsletter
  • Special events

Diamond $100+: Your gift of $100 or more per month will pay for two speech classes per month.

Ruby: Your gift of $75 per month will pay for one care partner support group each month, led by trained professionals.

Emerald: Your gift of $50 per month will pay for one exercise group per month – enhancing strength, balance, and community for people with PD.

Sapphire: Your gift of $25 per month will go towards one Dance for Movement Disorders class every month – a musical alternative for regular exercise.

Amethyst: Your gift of $10 per month will provide funds for exercise equipment for people with PD.
