Sponsor a DAPS Event or Newsletter

When you sponsor and attend a Dallas Area Parkinsonism Society event, you get a chance to experience the camaraderie that comes with our gatherings. You also have the opportunity to meet the people we serve, knowing that you are contributing to the continuation of the services that we provide for those dealing with the challenges of Parkinson's disease.

Sponsoring a newsletter lets the community know you support our mission and helps provide the funds to keep this valuable resource available by email and on our website.

Thank you for supporting our mission of impacting and improving the lives of those affected by Parkinson's disease!
